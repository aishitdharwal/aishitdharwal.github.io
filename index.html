🚀 Industry-Focused Curriculum

1. AI That Actually Works

Skip the hype. Learn what makes AI systems succeed or fail in production: bias-variance tradeoffs, attention mechanisms, and why most AI projects never make it to market.

2. Production-Ready Evaluation

Master the metrics that matter in real businesses. From ROC curves to monitoring data drift, learn how to prove your AI delivers actual business value, not just impressive demos.

3. AI Risk & Compliance

Navigate the regulatory landscape without getting sued. EU AI Act, bias detection, adversarial attacks - the unglamorous but critical skills that keep companies safe.

4. Strategic Model Decisions

API vs local deployment? GPT-4 vs Claude vs open-source? Learn the cost-benefit analysis frameworks that separate amateur hour from professional AI implementation.

5. Rapid AI Prototyping

Build functional AI products in days, not months. Master no-code tools (n8n, Retool, GlideApps) that let you validate ideas before writing a single line of code.

6. Advanced Implementation

Go beyond basic chatbots. Fine-tuning for specific domains, multimodal AI systems, and RAG architectures that actually improve over time with real user data.
